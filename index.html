Seamless, collaborative workflow designed for scalability

01

Initial Consultation

Our project manager works with the client to understand the annotation requirements and guidelines to propose solutions tailored to their needs.

02

Demo Annotation

We complete a set of sample annotations for the client to review and validate. This allows us to understand the challenges and potential edge cases before processing the full set of data.

03

Agreement

We finalize the terms and conditions and project pipeline with the client and proceed to sign the agreement.

04

Project Setup

We set up the project environment on the labeling platform and prepare a team of trained annotators for production.

05

Production

We initiate the annotation process as per the project guidelines with real-time quality control. Our project manager maintains continuous communication with the client and discusses any further edge-cases.

06

Export Results

We notify the client to review the results. Based on the feedback from the client, we make necessary adjustments before delivering the results.
